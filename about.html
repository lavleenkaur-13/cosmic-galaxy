Our Origin

BORN FROM A LATE NIGHT AND A STAR MAP

In 2021, our founder Arjun Mehta was an astrophysics student with a simple frustration — space was the most awe-inspiring thing in existence, but the clothes people wore to talk about it felt forgettable. Generic prints on cheap cotton. No soul, no story.

So he started sketching. Not rocket schematics — silhouettes. Hoodies that felt like they belonged in mission control. Tees that carried the weight of nebulae. Jackets inspired by the layered atmospheres of distant worlds.

Three friends, one Chandigarh apartment, and ₹40,000 of savings later — COSMIC GALAXY was launched. The first drop of 50 tees sold out in 11 hours.

What We Stand For

OUR MISSION & VALUES

DARE TO LOOK UP

Every piece we make is a reminder that curiosity is a superpower. We design for dreamers, explorers, and people who find wonder in the cosmos.

QUALITY THAT ORBITS

We use 100% organic cotton, space-grade dyes, and sustainable packaging. Every stitch is made to outlast trends and survive re-entry.

MADE FOR EARTH

Space inspires us, but Earth sustains us. Every order plants a tree. Our packaging is 100% recycled. We're building a brand that leaves the planet better.

CREW FIRST

Our community isn't customers — they're crew members. Early access, exclusive drops, and a space to connect with fellow explorers around the world.

DESIGN WITH PURPOSE

We collaborate with actual astronomers, space agencies, and street artists to create collections that are rooted in science and alive with culture.

LIMITLESS VISION

The universe has no ceiling. Neither do we. From Chandigarh to the cosmos — we're building a brand with no edge to its ambition.
