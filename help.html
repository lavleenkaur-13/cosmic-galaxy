SIZE GUIDE

FIND YOUR PERFECT FIT

Our garments are designed with a modern streetwear fit. Use the charts below to find your size. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for a more relaxed look or sizing down for a more fitted feel.

All measurements are in centimetres (cm). Measure yourself and compare to the garment measurements below, not your body measurements directly.

FIT TYPES

OVERSIZED FIT
Dropped shoulders, extended body. Our hoodies and some tees use this fit. Size down for a relaxed fit, keep your size for a true oversized look.
REGULAR FIT
Comfortable through the chest and shoulders. True to size. Most of our T-shirts and jackets use this fit.
SLIM FIT
Tailored through the body. If you have a broader chest or arms, size up. Featured in select tees and jackets.

HOW TO MEASURE

CHEST
Wrap the tape measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping it parallel to the ground and under your arms.
WAIST
Measure around your natural waist — typically 2–3 cm above your belly button. Keep the tape comfortably snug but not tight.
INSEAM
Measure from the crotch seam down to the ankle bone on the inside of the leg. Best measured from a pair of well-fitting trousers.

Still unsure? DM us on Instagram @cosmicgalaxy with your measurements and the product name — our team will personally recommend the right size for you.

ELIGIBLE FOR RETURN
  • Item received in wrong size or wrong product

  • Item significantly different from product description
  • Unused, unworn items within 7 days with tags intact

NOT ELIGIBLE

FAQ

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Quick answers to the questions we get asked the most. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team directly.

How do I track my order?
Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive an SMS and email with your tracking number and a link to our courier partner's website. You can track your order in real time. If you haven't received a tracking link within 2 business days of placing your order, contact us.
Can I modify or cancel my order after placing it?
You can cancel or modify your order within 2 hours of placing it, before it enters processing. After that, we're unable to make changes as the order has already been packed. Contact us immediately at [email protected] with your Order ID.
I placed an order but haven't received a confirmation email. What should I do?
First, check your spam/junk folder. If it's not there, the email address entered may have been incorrect. Contact us with your name, phone number and approximate order time and we'll locate your order.
Can I place a bulk or wholesale order?
Yes! For orders of 10+ pieces, we offer wholesale pricing. Email us at [email protected] with the products, quantities, and sizes you need and we'll send you a custom quote within 24 hours.
How long does delivery take?
Standard delivery takes 3–5 business days across India. Express delivery (1–2 days) is available in major metros. Remote areas (NE states, J&K, islands) may take up to 7–8 business days.
Is there free shipping?
Yes! All orders above ₹999 qualify for free standard shipping. Orders below ₹999 incur a flat ₹99 shipping fee. Use code COSMIC10 for 10% off your first order — this may also push you above the free shipping threshold!
Do you ship internationally?
We currently ship within India only. International shipping is in our roadmap — follow us on Instagram @cosmicgalaxy to be the first to know when it launches!
My order hasn't arrived yet. What do I do?
First, check your tracking link. If the tracking shows "delivered" but you haven't received it, check with neighbours and your building security. If the tracking hasn't updated for more than 3 business days, contact us and we'll investigate immediately.
What fabric/material are your products made from?
We use premium quality materials across our range. T-shirts are 100% combed cotton (180–200 GSM), hoodies are 80% cotton / 20% polyester fleece (320–380 GSM), jackets are 100% polyester or denim depending on the style, and cargo pants are 100% cotton twill (280 GSM). Full material details are listed on each product page.
How should I wash and care for my COSMIC GALAXY products?
T-shirts: Machine wash cold, inside out, gentle cycle. Tumble dry low. Do not bleach or dry clean.

Hoodies: Machine wash cold, inside out. Air dry or tumble dry low. Avoid high heat — it can shrink the fabric.

Jackets: Follow the care label — most are hand wash or dry clean only.

General tip: Washing inside out preserves print quality significantly longer.
Will the prints fade after washing?
Our prints use DTG (Direct to Garment) or screen printing with water-based inks. When washed inside out in cold water, the prints are extremely durable and will not fade significantly for at least 50+ washes. Avoid washing in hot water or drying on high heat.
A product I want is out of stock. Will it come back?
We do restock popular items! Join our newsletter or follow us on Instagram to be notified of restocks. You can also email us with the product name and your size — we track demand and prioritise restocks accordingly.
What is your return policy?
We offer a 7-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags attached. See our full Returns page for details.
How do I get a refund?
Once we receive your returned item and inspect it (usually 1 business day), we initiate the refund immediately. It reaches your account in 3–7 business days depending on your bank. You'll receive a confirmation email when the refund is processed.
Can I exchange for a different size?
Yes! We offer free size exchanges within 7 days. Email us with your Order ID, current size, and the size you want. We'll arrange a pickup and dispatch the new size once we receive your return. Subject to availability.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM), Credit/Debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, RuPay, Amex), Net Banking (all major Indian banks), and Cash on Delivery. All online payments are 100% encrypted and secure.
Is it safe to use my card on your website?
Absolutely. Our payment gateway is PCI-DSS compliant and uses 256-bit SSL encryption. We never store your card details on our servers. All transactions are secured and processed by trusted payment partners.
My payment failed but money was deducted. What happens?
If your payment failed but the amount was deducted, it is typically a bank hold and will be automatically reversed within 5–7 business days. If it's not reversed, email us at [email protected] with your transaction reference and we'll resolve it immediately.
Do you offer EMI?
EMI is available on select credit cards for orders above ₹2,000. EMI options (3, 6, 12 months) will appear during checkout if your card is eligible. No-cost EMI is available on select cards — standard bank processing fees may apply.
