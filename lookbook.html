THE MAKING OF SS'25

SHOT AMONG
THE STARS

Season 3 was conceived at 3 AM with a star map and a fever dream. We shot over 5 nights on location — urban rooftops, observatory terraces, and open desert — chasing the exact quality of light that exists between dusk and infinity.

Every look was assembled in collaboration with astronauts, artists, and people who simply believe in dressing as if the universe is watching.
